## Current Projects

### LocalKonnect (In Progress)
A mobile app concept connecting small sellers to buyers in local communities.

**Problem:** Small business owners (like me) struggle to reach nearby customers efficiently.

**Goal:** Create a simple, intuitive platform for local buying and selling.

**Progress:** User research complete | Personas defined | Wireframes in progress

### TikTok Content as Design Lab
I treat each TikTok video as a mini design project, testing:
- Visual hooks and attention-grabbers
- Content flow and storytelling
- Call-to-action placement
- User engagement metrics

What I learn directly applies to UX design principles.
